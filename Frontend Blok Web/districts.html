Discover Porto through the eyes of those who live here every day.

Like A Local Likes takes you on a journey through Porto, guided by those who know it best: the locals. Each episode reveals the secrets and routines of a special district, offering you an authentic perspective of the city.

  • Republic District

    Maria Martinho, Press & PR Consultant

    Maria lives in the Republic District, where she truly feels at home. Among shops rich in history, restaurants full of tradition, and the modernity of new buildings, this is where she finds the perfect balance for a lifestyle that is both exciting and serene. Shall we discover the secrets of the Republic District?

  • Atlantic District

    Helder Pacheco, Professor and Historian, and Gaspar Leite, Founder of the Alavanca Festival

    Two complementary perspectives: Hélder Pacheco unveils the memories and hidden stories of Foz; while Gaspar Leite o ffers a contemporary gaze, sharing lived experiences and inspiring seaside corners.

  • University District

    Francisco Porto Fernandes, President of the Porto Academic Federation.

    Francisco Porto Fernandes truly embodies the academic spirit of the city and knows the University District like no one else – from the quiet libraries perfect for studying to the gardens that offer a breath of fresh air between classes, from cultural venues to the most popular student canteens and academic traditions.

  • Boavista District

    Filipe Fangueiro and Paulo Meixedo, founders of Dsection Magazine.

    Filipe Fangueiro and Paulo Meixedo live and work in Boavista. Some of the most creative ideas for the fashion magazine Dsection come from walks through its streets and gardens. Shall we uncover the secrets of the Boavista District together? .

  • Bonfim District

    Agustina Fortunato, co-owner of AA Certain Café.

    Born in Argentina, Agustina Fortunato chose Bonfim District to live and work. The district returns the favour with energy, authenticity, and creative spirit at every corner. Shall we explore Bonfim District together?
